Interactive Narrative HW

My homunculus is an unfortunate character. Her life has been full of tragedy and the terrible luck has followed her since. Even if she tries to lead a normal life, things don't go her way. She's pretty lazy when it comes to stuff that doesn't interest her but when it comes to protecting her friends, she will try her best. She won't usually kill people, thus the stun gun. But if anyone close to her dies, she'll get revenge. Although she'll eventually regret it.
